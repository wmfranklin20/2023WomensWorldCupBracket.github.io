2023 P&W Women's World Cup Bracket Challenge
Welcome to the 2023 PW Bracket Challenge! Below is an interactive bracket for you to set up your picks. Please start by first dragging the teams in each group into your predicted order, remember that only the top two teams advance from each group. After selecting your group stage advancers, start selecting the teams you believe will advance from each knockout stage all the way up to the Semi-Finals. Lastly, select the teams you believe will advance to the final, noting that the losers of the Semi's will move to the 3rd Place game. Select your winner, third place winner and input your predicted Final score and your bracket is done! Please take a screenshot of your bracket and send to me with your name!
