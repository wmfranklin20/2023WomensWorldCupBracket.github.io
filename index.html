FIFA Women's World Cup 2023 P&W World Cup Bracket Challenge Australia & New Zealand
Welcome to the 2023 P&W Women's World Cup Bracket Challenge! This year's tournament is taking place Down Under, hosted jointly by New Zealand and Australia. To replace the Bluebeam method used for the Men's World Cup, please use the interactive bracket below to predict the final bracket and winner!

To start, please pick the teams you believe will advance from each group by dragging the team tiles into your preferred order (first place at the top, fourth at the bottom). You should see the bracket populate with your selected teams.

Next, click on the team you believe will advance from each matchup, starting the Round of 16 and continuing up until the Semi-Finals.

Lastly, select your winning team and provide a predicted score for the final match as a tiebreaker. Also select the team you think will win the Third place match by clicking on them!

Once you have filled out your bracket please add your name and take a screenshot before sending to me! I will compile everyones picks and provide updated scoring as we go (too lazy to write a backend to the site so Google Sheets it is)!
